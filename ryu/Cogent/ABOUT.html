ABOUT

MANAGEMENT

Jun Iinuma

Co-Founder & Chairman

As the thirteenth employee of Salesforce.com, Jun Iinuma contributed to its rapid growth from its inception and to the SaaS industry for over 15 years. There he led the proposals of new business solutions and business applications for a wide range of industries and enterprises. Jun co-founded Cogent Labs with the strong belief in the potential of the utilization of cutting-edge technologies and Deep Learning to change and benefit society.

Eric Hideyuki Whiteway

Co-Founder & CEO

Prior to founding Cogent Labs, Eric Whiteway was a Managing Director at Morgan Stanley for 16 years where he led Risk Trading and Proprietary Algorithmic Trading in Japan. An alumnus of Columbia University, he studied Computer Science and Operations Research. Excited by the advances and prospects of Deep Learning and its potential applications, Eric co-founded Cogent Labs in 2014.

Min Suk Song

Chief Operating Officer

Min Suk Song previously worked in Samsung Electronics for over 17 years, leading various AI R&D projects as a Principal Engineer transforming advanced academic research into practical and scalable commercial services. He holds a bachelor and a master of science degree from Columbia University in Computer Science. In addition he holds a master of business administration from Indiana University.

Kyoji Kimoto

Chief Financial Officer

Kyoji Kimoto has 20 years of experience in audit, financial consulting and corporate investment, including a tenure at Deloitte Tohmatsu FAS and Deutsche Securities, as well as founding a financial advisory business. He has supported numerous IPOs and managed more than 50 M&A deals. Kyoji previously served as corporate auditor for Cogent Labs prior to joining as CFO in March of 2018.

Minoru Hoshina

Chief Revenue Officer

At Oracle Corporation Japan, as an executive officer from August 2000, and as a managing director from June 2006, Hoshina Minoru held important positions in product marketing, support services, industry sales, alliance sales, etc. After joining Salesforce.com Japan in 2008 as a senior executive officer, he oversaw a wide range of divisions, including alliances, sales engineers, consulting services, and support, managing various large-scale projects such as Japan Post Group, and Toyota Friend. Hoshina Joined Cogent Labs as CRO in February 2019.

Keiji Torii

Audit & Supervisory Board Member

Joined Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank, Ltd. (Currently Mizuho Financial Group) since 1971, studied in London and Switzerland for his MBA degree. He brings rich management experience, from Managing Director of Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank, Ltd. from 2000, Representative Director & Deputy President of Mizuho Financial Group, Inc. from 2004, and Director & Deputy President of Mizuho Information & Research Institute, Inc. from 2005. Also served as Audit & Supervisory Board Member of ITOCHU Corporation and Director Board Member of Tokyo Coca-Cola Bottling Co., Ltd., and as Executive Advisor to Salesforce.Com. Torii joined Cogent Labs as Audit & Supervisory Board Member in March of 2019.
